Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

At Satin & Stem (“we”, “us”, “our”), we respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you visit our website satinandstem.shop or interact with us.

1. Information we collect

When you place an order we collect your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information (processed securely by Stripe).

When you sign up for our newsletter we collect your email address.

When you apply to The Stem Collective we collect your name, social media handles, follower count, and any other information you provide.

Automatically we may collect IP address, browser type, and usage data through cookies (to improve our site).

2. How we use your information

  • Process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and payment.
  • Send you order confirmations, shipping updates, and customer service messages.
  • Send you marketing emails (only if you opt in – you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Evaluate and manage influencer applications.
  • Improve our website and product offerings.

3. Sharing your information

We never sell your personal data. We share it only with:

  • Service providers (Stripe for payments, Supabase for database, Resend for emails, Shippo for shipping labels).
  • Legal obligations if required by law.

4. Your choices

  • You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by clicking the link in any email.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at satinandstem@protonmail.com.

5. Data retention

We retain order information for as long as necessary to fulfill legal obligations (e.g., tax records). Newsletter and influencer data are kept until you unsubscribe or ask to be removed.

6. Children’s privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

8. Contact us

If you have any questions, email us at satinandstem@protonmail.com.
